The learned counsel for the petitioner mainly contended that the petitioner was transferred thricewithin a short span of period and further, the transfer orders are issued at the instance of the fifthrespondent, who is the President of Parent-Teacher Association.3. According to the petitioner, the fifth respondent is frequently raising unnecessary allegationsagainst the petitioner, who is working as a Graduate Teacher and the Authorities are alsotransferring the petitioner on deputation. The petitioner submitted several complaints against thefifth respondent, but no action was taken by the Authority concerned and hence, the petitioner isconstrained to move this writ petition.4. The petitioner is working as Graduate Teacher from the year 2012 onwards in RamanathapuramDistrict. The petitioner being a Government School Teacher is liable to be transferred wheneverrequired, if administrative decision is taken by the Authorities competent.S.Jeeva vs The Director Of Education on 21 February, 2022Indian Kanoon - http://indiankanoon.org/doc/51701627/25. A perusal of the earlier transfer order dated 03.09.2018 reveals that the petitioner was transferredfrom Sathankulam to Vaalantharavai on deputation in the interest of the students. Again, thepetitioner was ____________ https://www.mhc.tn.gov.in/judis W.P.(MD) No.21509 of 2019retransferred to Sathankulam vide proceedings dated 11.04.2019. Therefore, the said transfer withina short span of period in the interest of the students cannot be construed as a regular transfer. Theteahcers are posted on deputation whenever exgicencies arise and in the interest of the students,who are studying in various Schools. The impugned order of transfer has been passed in the interestof the students.6. The petitioenr states that she filed several complaints against the fifth respondent, who is thePresident of Parent-Teacher Association as he raised unnecessary allegations against the petitioner.In such circumstances, the Authority competent is bound to conduct enquiry in respect of theallegations and initiate appropriate action. Whenever a complaint is filed against the teachers or theother persons, the Authority competent is bound to look into the complaints and take allappropriate actions in the manner known to law. No doubt, a Government employee may betransferred in lieu of suspension. A Government employee may be transferred on administrativegrounds. Transfers on deputation are issued in order to meet out certain exigencies. This being thecondition of service, the employee cannot challenge the order of transfer in a routine manner.____________ https://www.mhc.tn.gov.in/judis W.P.(MD) No.21509 of 20197. A writ against the order of transfer may be entertained by the High Courts, only if such an order isissued by the incompetent Authority or allegation of mala fide is raised. The allegation made by thepetitioner as against the fifth respondent is totally unconnected with malice or official mala fides.However, this Court is of the considered opinion that the petitioner has raised some allegationsagainst the fourth respondent Headmaster also. In such circumstances, the first respondent isdirected to depute a responsible person to find out the happenings of the Sathankulam GovernmentHigher Secondary School and with reference to the allegations and counter allegations raisedbetween the parties, to initiate all appropriate steps in order to ensure that the students get propereducation in the School because, due to these sort of allegations, the interest of the students at nopoint of time should be affected and the school discipline and the students' interest are allparamount interest of the Government School. Under these circumstances, the first respondent isdirected to look into the matter and initiate all appropriate actions against the officials and teachersand the employees concerned for the purpose of adminstering the Government School in a propermanner.8. Regarding the impugned order, it was issued in the year 2019 and under these circumstances, anadministrative decision is to be taken by ____________ https://www.mhc.tn.gov.in/judisW.P.(MD) No.21509 of 2019 the Authority competent in this regard. The Court cannot issue anydirection to post a Government employee in a particular post or place and the employees have noright to claim any such place or post as the transfer is incidental to service more so, a condition ofservice.9. With these observations and directions, the writ petition is disposed of. No costs.
